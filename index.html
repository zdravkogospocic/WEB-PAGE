Creating AI Football analyss

Train the camera well to follow the player and count the number of lost balls per game and how well the player sticks to the formation Our solution would help clubs, coaches and players to better understand formation errors for the whole team, and in general for an individual player, how much he sticks to the given formation and how many balls he lost It also offers savings as most clubs have to have many chips and expensive infrastructure to track players and formations, and we would solve that with a single camera

Problem to solve

The problem is that clubs, coaches and players spend a lot of resources on infrastructure, chips and advanced technology for analysis and statistics. Also, each individual player wants to improve himself as much as possible, and he does not have such detailed and trained camera vision to focus only on himself and his formation and quality of play.
